University of Ruhuna


University of Ruhuna marks its Excellency as one of the oldest and largest Universities in Sri Lanka while ranking up for the 1stplace in 2017. University of Ruhuna encapsulates a rich multicultural environment including students from diverse ethnic groups of Sri Lanka and international students. It is also known as the Garden University of Sri Lanka with an area of 72 hectares below the Matara beach range and banks of the Nilwala river in Sri Lanka.
